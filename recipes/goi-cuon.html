Goi Cuon

Ingredients

  • Rice paper
  • Shrimp (cooked)
  • Fresh herbs (mint, cilantro)
  • Rice vermicelli noodles
    • ÃŸ
  • Peanut sauce
  • Lettuce leaves

Steps

  1. Soak rice paper in warm water until soft.
  2. Place shrimp, herbs, noodles, and lettuce on the rice paper.
  3. Roll tightly and serve with peanut sauce.
  4. Enjoy your fresh spring rolls!
