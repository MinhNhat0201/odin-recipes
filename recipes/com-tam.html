Com Tam

Ingredients

  • Broken rice
  • Pork chop (grilled)
  • Pickled vegetables
  • Fried egg
  • Fish sauce
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Cook the broken rice according to package instructions.
  2. Grill the pork chop until cooked through.
  3. Prepare pickled vegetables by soaking them in vinegar and sugar.
  4. Fry an egg to your liking.
  5. Serve the rice topped with grilled pork, pickled vegetables, and fried egg.
