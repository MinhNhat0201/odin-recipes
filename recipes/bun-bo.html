Bun Bo

Ingredients

  • Rice vermicelli noodles
  • Beef (thinly sliced)
  • Fresh herbs (mint, cilantro, basil)
  • Bean sprouts
  • Chopped peanuts
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar
  • Lime juice

Steps

  1. Cook the rice vermicelli noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a bowl, mix fish sauce, sugar, and lime juice to create the dressing.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked noodles, beef, fresh herbs, and bean sprouts.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss well.
  5. Top with chopped peanuts before serving.

Com Tam

Ingredients

  • Broken rice
  • Pork chop (grilled)
  • Pickled vegetables
  • Fried egg
  • Fish sauce
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Cook the broken rice according to package instructions.
  2. Grill the pork chop until cooked through.
  3. Prepare pickled vegetables by soaking them in vinegar and sugar.
  4. Fry an egg to your liking.
  5. Serve the rice topped with grilled pork, pickled vegetables, and fried egg.

Goi Cuon

Ingredients

  • Rice paper
  • Shrimp (cooked)
  • Fresh herbs (mint, cilantro)
  • Rice vermicelli noodles
  • Peanut sauce
  • Lettuce leaves

Steps

  1. Soak rice paper in warm water until soft.
  2. Place shrimp, herbs, noodles, and lettuce on the rice paper.
  3. Roll tightly and serve with peanut sauce.
  4. Enjoy your fresh spring rolls!
