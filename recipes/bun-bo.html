Bun Bo

Ingredients

  • Rice vermicelli noodles
  • Beef (thinly sliced)
  • Fresh herbs (mint, cilantro, basil)
  • Bean sprouts
  • Chopped peanuts
  • Fish sauce
  • Sugar
  • Lime juice

Steps

  1. Cook the rice vermicelli noodles according to package instructions.
  2. In a bowl, mix fish sauce, sugar, and lime juice to create the dressing.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cooked noodles, beef, fresh herbs, and bean sprouts.
  4. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss well.
  5. Top with chopped peanuts before serving.
